1. SERVICES AND THE USE OF THE PLATFORM
1.1 No service pursuant to this Agreement will be provided by the Company to the Investor unless the Investor is a person or entity prescribed as a “Professional Investor” as defined under Part 1 of Schedule 1 of the SFO.
1.2 The Company shall, from the date of the signing of this Agreement, provide the Investor access to its online platform on its online website (the “Platform”) whereby the Company would, in accordance with the instructions and preferences to be set by the Investor in the Platform, use its fund-matching system to search for potential money borrowers which fulfils the criteria and/or preferences set by the Investor and lend the whole or any part of the fund deposited by the Investor into the Company’s designated bank account (the “Auto Matching System”) under the name of the Company to the potential borrower, thereby bridging one or multiple loan agreements between the Company on one part using the Investor’s fund on one part and the potential borrower on another part (the “Loan”).
1.3 Upon being registered as a lender on the Platform, having duly completed the prescribed form(s) and declaration(s) which require the Investor to provide the information as required by the Company pursuant to the form(s) and declaration(s), the Investor shall have access to his personal account (“Zwap Account”) showing details of his finance and personal information, but the Company shall not have any obligations to disclose to the Investor any personal details of the borrowers to which his fund deposited with the Company has been lent.
1.4 Before being eligible to use the Auto Matching System on the Platform, the Investor shall ensure that he fulfils the criteria of eligibility to be set by the Company from time to time.
1.5 The commencement and/or cessation of operation of the Auto Matching System shall be subject to the instructions given by the Investor in the Platform.
1.6 The Company shall carry out reasonable risk assessments in approving a potential borrower’s application to become a registered borrower on the Platform but the Company shall not be liable for any fraudulent or untrue information supplied by a potential borrower.
1.7 The Company has not rendered and will not render to the Investor any advice or suggestion in relation to any sorts of investments whatsoever.
1.8 The Company shall be responsible for the management, operation and the maintenance of the Platform.
1.9 The Company may change at any time, with notice to the Investor as is reasonably practicable and sufficient:
(a) the content, format or nature of the services provided as hereinafter described; and
(b) the means of access to the Platform.
1.10 In case of any default in repayment of the Loan:-
(a) the Company shall be entitled to appoint third party recovery agents to assist in the recovering and the procuring of repayment by the borrower without the need to seeking the Investor’s consent. Any cost thereof shall be borne by the borrower but in case such cost is irrecoverable it shall be borne by the Company; and
(b) the Company reserves the right to initiate legal proceedings against the defaulting borrower for the recovery of loan. Any cost thereof shall be borne by the borrower but in case such cost is irrecoverable it shall be borne by the Company.


2. THE LOAN, RETURN OF INVESTMENT AND RISK
2.1 The Company shall have absolute discretion to enter into a loan agreement with potential borrowers at any terms the Company deems fit provided always that such terms and conditions shall not contradict with the criteria and preferences set by the Investor in the Platform.
2.2 The Investor shall be entitled to receive interest from the Company as return for the investment and the actual rate of the interest shall be provided in the Platform.
2.3 The Investor acknowledges and agrees that the actual return of investment shall depend on the due repayment of the Loan on the part of the borrowers in respective loan agreements entered with the Company, and in the event of default, Clause 2.5 below shall apply and the Investor may not receive the estimated return or any return at all.
2.4 In the event that a borrower repays the Loan by early repayment pursuant to the terms and conditions of the said borrower’s loan agreement entered into with the Company, the Company shall, within three (3) business days from the full early repayment of the Loan, deposit into the Investor’s Zwap Account his contribution to the principal of the Loan together with interest accrued up to the date of early repayment at the actual rate provided in the Platform.
2.5 In the event that a borrower defaults repayment of the Loan, the Company shall, not later than 18 months from the borrower’s default and after deducting any expenses or legal costs incurred by the Company incidental to and in connection with the Company’s debt recovery attempt, deposit into the Investor’s Zwap Account the money repaid by the borrower (if any) proportionate to the Investor’s contribution to the principal of the Loan.


3. TERMINATION AND WITHDRAWAL OF FUNDS
3.1 Without affecting any other right or remedy available to it, either Party to this Agreement may terminate the Agreement by giving five (5) business days’ notice in writing to the other Party.
3.2 Within one (1) business day from the date of termination, the Company shall return to the Investor the balance of the funds remaining in the Investor’s Zwap Account. If, at the time of termination, part of the said balance has been lent to any borrowers by the Company, the Company shall only be obliged to return the sum(s) lent to borrowers to the Investor pursuant to Clauses 2.4 or 2.5 above, as the case may be. Such sums shall be paid to the Investor within three (3) business days from the day such sum is paid into the Investor’s Zwap Account in accordance with Clauses 2.4 or 2.5 above, as the case may be.
3.3 The Investor may also from time to time withdraw the whole or part of the balance of funds remaining in his Zwap Account by giving the Company notice in writing provided that the Investor shall not be entitled to withdraw any sum which has been lent to a borrower by the Company. The Company shall pay the Investor such sum requested to be withdrawn within seven (7) business days of the receipt of such notice from the Investor.
3.4 The Investor may also from time to time place funds into the Zwap Account for the purposes of maintaining sufficient funds in the Zwap Account as required under Clause 1.4 above.
